Logo Usage – Do & Don't

Keep the logo clear, simple, and aligned with Chrono's brand identity.

Do

  • Use on high-contrast backgrounds.
  • Keep proportions intact.
  • Use official color palette.

Don't

  • Don't distort or stretch the logo.
  • Don't change logo colors.
  • Don't use on busy backgrounds.
